Camping Reservations

You asked. We listened.
A new camping reservation experience is coming to BC Parks this year.

Thank you for visiting the BC Parks’ in-development new Camping Reservation Service

After listening to users over the past two seasons, including conducting over 100 user experience research interviews informing this work, BC Parks is preparing to launch a new reservation service for the 2022 season. This new service, now tied directly to bcparks.ca, will include a number of upgrades including: more flexible search options to find and book a campsite; a pre-booking queuing system; as well as handy maps, calendars and lists of available sites for quicker navigation.

Our new reservation service will be available for bookings starting March 21st, 2022 at 7:00 am PT. Come back here in advance, starting March 15th, 2022, to preview the reservation service, create your new account (required to enable this new service), and explore online.

In the meantime, feel free to visit us at bcparks.ca/reserve for more information on camping reservations for the upcoming season, or bcparks.ca/explore to begin planning your next visit.

We look forward to welcoming you in park soon!
